I-MAT PROJECT

Innovative Mobility Attracting Tools for Latin America

ABOUT
I-MAT & ERASMUS+

Starting 2023, and for a period of three years, nine HEIs from 6 different countries will be working on identifying the needs or support required and the priorities of the three countries Latin American countries (Brazil, Colombia, and Peru), and the participating HEIs; and matching these to the priorities and relevant expertise of the Programme Country HEIs (Lithuania, Poland and Portugal) we believe I-MAT will maximize the benefit to these countries in general and the partner HEIs, in particular with the stamp of the European Commission.
